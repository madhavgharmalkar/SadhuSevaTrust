About Us

Founded in 2017, Sadhu Seva Trust is an initiative to help the residents of Vrindavan who are in need of food, clothing, shelter, and support. Vrindavan is a holy city in India which attracts hundreds upon thousands of pilgrims every year from all over the world.

Run by young philanthropists who have lived in Vrindavan for more than 15 years combined, the founders of the Sadhu Seva Trust have a vision to give back to the people who have taken care of them in a place they affectionately now call home. The organisation is based in Vrindavan and therefore is physically and directly able to provide those in need.

This Charity is a small endeavour to serve the residents of Vrindavan, it is dedicated to His Divine Grace A.C Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada.
